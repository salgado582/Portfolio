Work History

"Success is the sum of small efforts, repeated day in and day out"

Fiverr/ Upwork

After I left the army, I was not interested in attending college right away, in fact, I was hesitant to go back to school due to the agen and experience difference between my classmates and I. I still had to do something for money so I picked up a full time job as an EMT for Atlanta while I picked up a new source of income. That's when i discovered freelancing on websites like Upwork and Fiverr. You mean to tell me that I can work from home and my clientele comes to me??? Where do I sign up!?

I have always been good with computers so I figured I would try my hand at it. My first inclination was SEO. It seemed like the best avenue to sink my teeth into. I posted my services on Fiverr first and worried about the skills portion as I get to them. My first client was in 2017, Gwinnetthairtransplant.com, a local hair transplant doctor looking to increase his search volume. I managed to get him to the bottom of the first page of google within a month! Unfortunately, I did not see this as a meaningful career opportunity because I focused on becoming a Surgeon to make my family proud.

My longest running Upwork project was for a website called Adventurous Kate This was for a former Microsoft employee that quit her job to travel and has never looked back since. My services to her included:

  • SEO
  • Blog creation and scheduling
  • Social media management
  • Email Management

BestLyfe Group

This is my current place of employment. I was hired at the beginning of 2021 as an SEO and front end developer. I actually had no intent of applying but my best friend who is a back end developer gave me the push I needed to apply to a job I knew I wanted but was to insecure about my skills to actually apply for. I will always be grateful to him for giving the much needed nudge to follow a passion that I wasn't sure I was capable of performing. It is here that I discovered where my skill lie and where I could take them. The positive and inclusive envirnonment within this company gave me the time and encouragement to become better at my craft. I would learn at night and practice what I learned during the day. I started by learning only what was pertinent to make the team succeed but once most of it became second nature, I began to branch out and learn more and more IT skills that I was confident my team would need in the future.

My resposibilities Include:

  • SEO research, analytics, implementation, & reporting.
  • Back end responsive web section design
  • Front end category and city page design.
  • Vlog/content creation
  • Client relations & trouble shooting
