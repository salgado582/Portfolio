San Diego Kids Party Rentals

Website Redesign, SEO, and Desperately Needed Help Getting Out of FaceBook Jail

San Diego Kids Party Rentals had massive inventory on their website entirely out of order, every page was a child page to the home page, items were out of categorical order, Website's distorted architecture made Googlebot exclude it from various search queries.

This Website was a team effort; My job was to reorganize the site's architecture to make it crawlable without exceeding Google Bot's crawl budget. This was done by reordering all the items to relevant category pages. Each category page was optimized with SEO by including specific keywords, external & internal hyperlinks, unique google maps with drawn delivery radiuss, FAQs, and city specific pages to strategically optimize their chosen cities. Markup was added to important pages. and index request was sent to google once site health was above 90%. Out of the 40 keywords per city for 5 cities, more than 30 made #1 in the first and second largest cities while the other 3 cities began to catch traction within two months after indexing request to Google.

I chose to showcase this one above my independently made websites because it highlights our cooperation as a team to create something that our client called "one of the best sites he's seen in the business"!
